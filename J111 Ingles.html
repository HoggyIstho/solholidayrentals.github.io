One bedroom apartment

The location of this bright and sunny apartment within Jupiter means that it is in the center of all the resort amenities and only a ten minute walk from the beach. The town of Arroyo de la Miel, the Tivoli World amusement park, the impressive Benalmádena marina and La Paloma park are all within walking distance.

The nearest golf course and casino are 2 km away. On site we have 24 hour English speaking reception, hairdresser, fantastic swimming pool and licensed poolside bar, WIFI can be hired through reception and is available in the apartment (signal may vary).

The pool is protected by lifeguards during the mid / high season. Across the street from the complex is Plaza Bonanza, with its many restaurants, bars and cafes. There is a money exchange shop.

The complex is just 11 km from Malaga airport.
